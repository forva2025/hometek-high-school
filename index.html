About Hometek High School

Building tomorrow's leaders through quality education

Our History

Established in 2010, Hometek High School has been a beacon of educational excellence in Soroti, Kampala, Uganda. For over 14 years, we have consistently delivered quality education that prepares students for success in higher education and their future careers.

Our Mission

To provide quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary for academic excellence and responsible citizenship.

Our Vision

To be a leading center of educational excellence in Uganda, fostering innovation, critical thinking, and character development.

Core Values

Excellence Integrity Innovation Respect Responsibility

Academic Excellence

Comprehensive education programs designed for success

Science Stream

Physics, Chemistry, Biology, and Mathematics - preparing students for careers in medicine, engineering, and technology.

Arts Stream

Literature, History, Geography, and Economics - fostering critical thinking and analytical skills.

Technical Stream

Technical Drawing, Woodwork, and Metalwork - hands-on learning for practical skills development.

Computer Studies & ICT

Modern technology education preparing students for the digital age and 21st-century careers.

Languages

English, Luganda, and Swahili - building communication skills for global citizenship.

Uganda Curriculum

Following the 7-4-2 education system with O-Level and A-Level programs for comprehensive development.

Admission Requirements

  • Primary Leaving Examination (PLE) Certificate
  • Birth Certificate
  • Passport Photos (4 copies)
  • Medical Report
  • Completed Application Form
  • Application Fee Payment
