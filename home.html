About Hometek High School

Our History & Vision

Founded in 2003, Hometek High School has grown to become one of Uganda's premier educational institutions. Our mission is to provide a transformative educational experience that prepares students for academic success and responsible global citizenship.

Mission

To nurture intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and ethical leadership through innovative education.

Vision

To be East Africa's leading high school in academic excellence and character development.

Values

Integrity, Excellence, Innovation, Community, and Respect.
